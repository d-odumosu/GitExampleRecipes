What you need
=============

* 1/4 cup sugar
* 1/4 cup flour
* 2 tbsp cocoa
* A pinch of salt
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 3 tbsp water
* oreo

Steps
=====

1. Add all the dry ingredients to a mug.
2. Add the oil and water.
3. Mix until everything is mixed together well.
4. Microwave for 1 minute 40 seconds.
5. topping; oreo
